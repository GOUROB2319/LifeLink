Our Vision

To create a Bangladesh where no one dies due to lack of blood. We envision a future where every patient in need has immediate access to safe blood through our AI-powered matching system.

Our Mission

To revolutionize blood donation in Bangladesh by building the largest verified network of donors, leveraging technology to make blood donation accessible, efficient, and impactful for every Bangladeshi.

Our Story

rocket_launch

2022: The Beginning

LifeLink was founded by a group of medical students who witnessed firsthand the critical blood shortage in Bangladesh hospitals.

groups

2023: Rapid Growth

We onboarded our first 10,000 donors and partnered with 100+ hospitals across Dhaka division.

psychology_alt

2024: AI Integration

Launched our AI-powered matching system, reducing average response time from hours to minutes.

public

2025: Nationwide Expansion

Expanded operations to all 8 divisions of Bangladesh, saving thousands of lives monthly.
